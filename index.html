Blenhein STEM - Green Power


About us...

We are a club that runs in Blenheim High School, it is all about the Greenpower electric car building and racing. The aim of the club is to inspire students to design, get creative, build teamwork skills and have fun. In the races you need to try and get as many laps as possible with limited battery power.
